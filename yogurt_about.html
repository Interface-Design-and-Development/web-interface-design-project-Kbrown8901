Why Yogurt?

It all started with a tiny fridge, two jars of milk, and one wild idea during a finals week meltdown. Swirly's was born from late-night cravings and a desire to make something fresh, flavorful, and fun. We believe yogurt should be bold, inspiring, and never boring.
